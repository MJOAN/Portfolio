    Events App

    React, Redux Thunk, Firestore, Firebase and Cloud Hosting!

    Events web app for your business and colleagues to setup times to meet and talk about your business projects! This app uses Google Maps, React/Redux Thunk, Firebase, Firestore and was deployed on the Cloud with Firebase hosting! To ensure a good experience please register first! There are many features including infinite scroll, chat, maps, adding, joining, and canceling events, plus Google authentication. This started from a tutorial and for me to better learn Google Firebase!

    During deployment I learned about code splitting, and npm analyze which showed me all of the dependencies and their percentage in usage. I learned about Firebase Cloud Functions and how to createActivity and cancelActivity which were "events" users were engaging with on the app that Firebase was "listening" for. This app is still in development and key features to add are security, Facebook login, and being able to follow your colleagues. Please let me know what you think!
